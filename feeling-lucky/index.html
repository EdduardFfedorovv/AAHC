FEELING LUCKY?

Many of you are working on “living right” by trying to maintain healthy lifestyles. On October 20th the African American Health Coalition, Inc. is providing THREE opportunities to “look tight” by providing lifestyle makeovers courtesy of our partners at Macy’s.

Simply “Like us” on the African American Health Coalition, Inc. Facebook page and three lucky individuals will be selected to receive lifestyle makeovers, including clothes, hair and make-up!

Hurry because selection closes on October 1st. Score a lifestyle makeover with makeup consultation & styling by Macy’s and Abibat Durosimi of Tabiba Styles consultations. Lifestyle makeover winners will make their debut at the AAHC Wellness Village on October 20th at the Ambridge Event Center located at 1333 NE MLK just prior to the Macy’s fashion show.

$10*Score a $10 Macy’s Gift Card! Come to the Wellness Village to get important health screenings and many more exciting surprises including 1,000 $10.00 Macy’s gift cards, stop by the Macy’s Beauty Lounge to pick up your $10 Macy’s gift card! Don’t miss out!!!!! Tell all of your friends and we’ll see you on October 20th.*Limit one per person. While supplies last.
