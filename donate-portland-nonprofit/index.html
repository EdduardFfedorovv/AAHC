Donate

For more information about sponsorship opportunities, please call us at 503.413.1850 or contact: Corliss McKeever

Everyone can donate to the African American Health Coalition, Inc. Investing in building the capacity of the African American Health Coalition, Inc. will make a big difference in the health and well-being of Portland’s entire community.

As the African American Health Coalition, Inc. consistently fulfills it’s mission of health education, advocacy and research, community members are better equipped to take control of their own health and to improve their quality of life.

Donating to the AAHC in the form of cash donations or through volunteerism is a significant step towards making Portland’s African American community the healthiest in the nation. The AAHC cannot continue its work without your generous support.

To donate, click the Donate tab, or go to the Volunteer tab to complete the volunteer application. You can also mail your check or money order to African American Health Coalition, Inc.; 2800 N. Vancouver Ste. 100; Portland, Oregon 97227. For more information contact us at 503.413.1850.

AAHC is a 501(c) (3) non-profit organization. All donations, grants and gifts are tax deductible and help us sustain our vital work in the community.
