Physical Activity Pass!

Want to get back in the gym and begin living a healthy lifestyle? If so, come over to the African American Health Coalition (AAHC) to purchase your physical activity pass for a $25 quarterly fee. (April 1st 2014 to June 30th 2014) The pass is offered to African Americans born in the United States and over the age of 16. The membership grants access to either:

  • Matt Dishman Community Center or Charles Jordan Community Center/Columbia Pool.
  • Five Days a week. (Mornings or Evenings)
  • The pool and Water Aerobics Classes
  • Group X Class

Please visit our office and pick up your new physical activity pass today!
